OKC Thunder Franchise History

The OKC Thunder franchise is a NBA team that is in the Northwestern Divsion in the Western Confrence. They were the 6th seed in the western confrence and were elminated in the first round of the palyoffs by the 3rd seed Portland Trail Blazers. They have never one a champion title before, but in the last 10 years half of the player scoring titles have been by Oklahoma City players. The Thunder have actually only recently aquired it's new name and city, as it played in Seattle as the Supersonics.

Personal Opinons

My personal opinions on the Thunder are more positive then it is negative. The Thunder have been a dominant force in the league for years, but that changed this offseason. This offseason them losing their star backcourt: Russel Westbrook and Paul George. With a couple other changs to the team, they officialy declared themselves in rebuild mode, or where they try to get themselves the next big stars. A team does this by gaining a lot of picks, which the Thunder have. As a Thunder Fan I am fine with this as the team has had it's chance to win countless times, and our team just didn't cut it. My hopes are that this franchise will become legendary, and win countless championships with that title.
